#  Detecting Deadlocks at Runtime

> [!CAUTION]
> Deadlock detection was deprecated in PostSharp 2024.0 and removed in PostSharp 2026.0.

Deadlock detection was discontinued and is not distributed as part of PostSharp from version 2026.0 onward.
